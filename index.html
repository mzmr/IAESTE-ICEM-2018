Why should you join us?

ICEM is an annual event for both IAESTE trainees and members from all over the world. This annual event is a must in your calendar.

Come to Cracow to feel the magic when you cross the streets around the Old Town and cruise the Vistula River. The city is the only one of its kind, offering you rich historic and cultural heritage, astounding sites and wonderful atmosphere.

Our Local Committee gives you the chance to spend an unforgettable weekend by preparing various attractions, beginning with treasure hunting city game, ending with all night long parties.

Meet awesome people and make unforgettable memories!
